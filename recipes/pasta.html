Pasta

Delicious-looking pasta

Description

Cooking time

  • most dry pasta will cook in 8-12mins
  • fresh pasta cook as quickly as 1-5mins
  • large pasta like lasagna noodles can take up to 15mins

Ingredients and Equipment

  • 1lb uncooked pasta
  • A 6-8qt pot
  • 4-6qt cold water
  • 1-2 tablespoons of salt
  • A long handled heat-proof spoon
  • A colander for draining the pasta

Directions

  1. Bring the water to a full, rolling boil.
  2. Add salt to the boiling water.
  3. Stir gently.
  4. Cook your pasta at a rapid boil until it's tender but slightly firm to the bite.
  5. Drain the pasta in a colander.
  6. Serve pasta immediately.
