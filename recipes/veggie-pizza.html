Veggie Pizza

Delicious-looking veggie pizza

Description

  • cook time:
    • prep time: 25m
    • cook time: 10m
    • additional time: 1h5m
    • total time: 1h40m
  • servings: 16
  • yield: 1 pizza

Ingredients

  • 2 8oz packages refrigerated cresent rolls
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 8oz package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 1oz package ranch seasoning mix
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • .25 teaspoon garlic salt
  • 1.5 cups chopped fresh broccoli
  • .5 cup halved and thinly-sliced radishes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 stalk celery, thinly-sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Spray a jelly roll pan with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Press crescent roll dough into the prepared jelly roll pan to form a crust. Let stand 5 minutes. Pierce with a fork.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until dough is fully cooked and golden brown, about 10 minutes. Let cool completely.
  4. Combine sour cream, cream cheese, ranch seasoning mix, dill, and garlic salt in a medium mixing bowl.
  5. Spread the cream cheese mixture on top of cooled crust.
  6. Arrange broccoli, radish, onion, bell pepper, carrot, and celery on top of the cream cheese mixture.
  7. Cover and let chill for 1-2 hours. Cut chilled pizza into 16 squares to serve.
