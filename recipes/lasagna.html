Lasagna

Delicious-looking lasagna

Description

It will take about 3h15m to prepare this item.

  • Prep Time: 30m
  • Cook Time: 2h30m
  • Additional Time: 15m
  • Total Time: 3h15m

It should serve around 12 people.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
